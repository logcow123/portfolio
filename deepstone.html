Once you first start the Deep Stone Crypt you will be outside infront of a buidling where you will find fallen inside. Kill all the fallen and it will open a door where you can pull out your sparrow and continue until you find an airock. Once all your teamates arrive, the airlock will open and you can begin traversing the desolate snow area. The main mechanic of this area is, if you are outside the big orange bubble you will get stacks of "Frostbite". Once it reaches 10 stacks you die. The goal is to traverse through these oranges bubbles until you reach the end airlock
